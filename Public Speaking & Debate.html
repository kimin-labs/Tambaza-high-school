Public Speaking & Debate at Tambaza High School

At Tambaza High School, we believe that every student has a voice worth hearing. Our Public Speaking & Debate Club is where ideas clash, arguments are refined, and future leaders learn to articulate their thoughts with confidence and clarity.

Our Debate Achievements

  • National Debate Champions 2023 - Secondary Schools Category
  • Regional Oratory Competition Winners for 4 consecutive years
  • Best Speaker awards in multiple inter-school competitions
  • Represented Tanzania in East African Debate Championships

What We Offer

  • Weekly public speaking workshops and practice sessions
  • Debate training on argument construction and rebuttal techniques
  • Impromptu speaking exercises to build quick thinking skills
  • Mock parliamentary debates and competitive training
  • Voice modulation and body language coaching

Our club is more than just arguments - it's about building confidence, critical thinking, and the ability to express complex ideas effectively.
