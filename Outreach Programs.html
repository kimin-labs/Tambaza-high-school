Outreach Programs at Tambaza High School

At Tambaza High School, we believe education extends beyond the classroom. Our outreach programs connect students with real-world professionals and opportunities, bridging the gap between academic learning and practical application.

Guest Speaker Programs

We regularly host professionals from leading institutions to share their expertise and career insights:

  • CRDB Bank: Financial literacy sessions and career opportunities in banking
  • NBC Bank: Entrepreneurship workshops and business management skills
  • University Professors: Academic pathways and research opportunities
  • Industry Experts: Technology, engineering, and innovation trends

Community & Institutional Outreach Events

Our students actively participate in community development and institutional collaborations:

  • Environmental conservation projects in partnership with local communities
  • STEM education outreach to primary schools in Dar es Salaam
  • Health awareness campaigns with medical institutions
  • Technology workshops for community digital literacy

Educational Seminars & Workshops

We organize seminars that demonstrate how academic subjects translate into real-world applications:

  • "Mathematics in Business": How algebra and statistics apply to entrepreneurship
  • "Science for Innovation": Practical applications of Biology and chemistry
  • Language Skills in Professional World: Importance of communication in careers
  • "Financial Literacy": Budgeting, saving, and investment basics

Benefits of Studying Different Combinations

Learn how your subject combination can lead to financial success:

  • PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics): Engineering, technology, research careers with high earning potential
  • PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology): Medicine, biotechnology, environmental science opportunities
  • PGM (Geography, Mathematics): Urban planning, GIS, environmental management
  • ECA (Economic, Commerce, Accountancy): Business management, entrepreneurship, economics
  • HGL (History, Geography, Language): Law, journalism, public administration
