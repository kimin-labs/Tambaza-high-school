Student Requirements for PCB Combination

To join the Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) combination at Tambaza High School, students must meet specific academic requirements and demonstrate aptitude in science subjects. Below are the detailed requirements for admission into this prestigious program.

Academic Requirements:
  • Form Four Examination Results: Must have Division I or Division II in Form Four National Examination (CSEE)
  • Science Subjects: Must have passed Biology, Chemistry, and Physics with minimum grades of C
  • Mathematics: Must have passed Basic Mathematics with at least a D grade
  • English Language: Must have passed English with at least a D grade
  • Additional Science: Good performance in Additional Mathematics is an added advantage
Application Process:
  • Complete the school application form available online or at the admissions office
  • Submit certified copies of Form Four examination results
  • Provide birth certificate or other proof of age documentation
  • Submit two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Attend an interview with the science department faculty
Selection Criteria:
  • Academic performance in science subjects (50% weighting)
  • Performance in mathematics and English (25% weighting)
  • Interview assessment (25% weighting)
  • Overall academic consistency and improvement trend
Additional Considerations:
  • Demonstrated interest in science through extracurricular activities
  • Participation in science fairs, competitions, or projects
  • Recommendation letters from science teachers
  • Personal statement explaining interest in pursuing science
Important Dates:
  • Application period: January 15 - March 31 annually
  • Interview dates: April 15-30 annually
  • Selection results announced: May 15 annually
  • Orientation for new students: June 1 annually

For more information about the PCB combination or to inquire about specific cases, please contact our admissions office at admissions@tambaza.ac.tz or call +255 22 215 1234 during business hours.
